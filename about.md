# About Me

Hi, I'm **Asad Shamna** — a cybersecurity enthusiast and penetration testing practitioner with a deep interest in understanding how systems work and how they can be secured against real-world threats.

My work revolves around exploring vulnerabilities, analyzing security weaknesses, and learning how attackers think so that systems can be built and maintained more securely. I strongly believe that understanding offensive security is one of the best ways to strengthen defensive security.

Alongside cybersecurity, I have experience in **web development**, which gives me a strong understanding of how modern applications are designed, built, and deployed. This perspective allows me to approach security from both the **builder’s mindset and the attacker’s mindset**.

## Why I Share Cybersecurity Knowledge

Cybersecurity can often feel complex or difficult for beginners. One of the biggest challenges people face when learning security is the lack of clear, practical explanations of how attacks and defenses actually work.

Through this blog, my goal is to make cybersecurity concepts more approachable by sharing:

- Practical penetration testing techniques
- Realistic lab walkthroughs and write-ups
- Security tools and methodologies
- Lessons learned while exploring vulnerabilities

By documenting my research and experiences, I hope to help others who are beginning their journey in cybersecurity and provide useful insights to those who enjoy learning about ethical hacking.

## What You’ll Find on This Blog

This platform serves as a knowledge base where I share:

- Cybersecurity tips and techniques
- Penetration testing methodologies
- Security tool usage and walkthroughs
- Practical write-ups from security labs and challenges

Everything shared here is intended for **educational purposes and ethical security learning**.
